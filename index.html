Fractional Ownership Option

Non-Fungible Token (NFT) is a digital asset that represents ownership of a unique item or piece of content. Unlike other digital assets such as Bitcoin or Ethereum, NFTs are not interchangeable or divisible. This means that ownership can be transferred digitally, making it possible to own an asset without ever physically possessing it.

Monetize Digital Work

Each NFT is one-of-a-kind, making it unique and valuable. This is because NFTs are built on blockchain technology, which ensures that each token has a unique digital signature that verifies its authenticity.
